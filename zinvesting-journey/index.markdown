This section documents my process of learning investing and stock trading through the TRADEway Investing Program, personal study, chart practice, paper trading, and trade reviews.

My goal is not to showcase perfect trades, butinstead to document my learning process, mistakes, lessons learned, and progress toward becoming a disciplined investor. Also to build understanding, confidence, discipline, and consistent decision-making through a repeatable system.
