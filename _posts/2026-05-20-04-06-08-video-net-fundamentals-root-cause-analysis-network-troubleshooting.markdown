This walkthrough demonstrates a structured troubleshooting process used to identify, analyze, and resolve a network connectivity problem.

<!--more-->

---

## Troubleshooting Methodology

A structured troubleshooting approach includes:

1. Identify symptoms
2. Gather information
3. Test connectivity
4. Compare configurations
5. Develop a theory
6. Test the theory
7. Implement a solution
8. Verify functionality
9. Document results
