<h3><strong>My Study Approach: Observation, Interpretation, Application</strong></h3>
<hr>
<p>
Alongside my technical studies in networking, I am committed to a structured and intentional approach to studying the First Testament. I am beginning in Genesis and working through each book systematically using the <strong><a href="https://www.precept.org/study/precept-bible-study-method/" target="_blank">Precept Bible Study Method.</a><br></strong>
</p>
<p>
This approach emphasizes understanding the text through careful observation, thoughtful interpretation, and practical application. Rather than relying solely on external commentary, I engage directly with the text to build a deeper and more personal understanding.
</p>

<!--more-->
<hr>
<h3><strong>What Is Inductive Study?</strong></h3>
<p>The inductive method is built on three key steps:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Observation</strong> – Identifying what the text says by noting key words, themes, and structure</li>
  <li><strong>Interpretation</strong> – Understanding what the text means in its proper context</li>
  <li><strong>Application</strong> – Applying the insights to personal growth and daily life</li>
</ul>
<p>
This process encourages active learning, critical thinking, and consistency—skills that also directly support my development in technical fields like networking.
</p>
