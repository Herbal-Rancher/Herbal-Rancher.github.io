This guided technical walkthrough demonstrates how to verify IPv4 and IPv6 addressing, test end-to-end connectivity, and analyze routing paths in a dual-stack network using Cisco Packet Tracer.

## Why This Matters

Modern enterprise networks commonly operate in dual-stack environments where IPv4 and IPv6 coexist. Understanding how to verify addressing, test connectivity, and trace packet paths using both protocols prepares network administrators to deploy, troubleshoot, and support today's enterprise infrastructures.
