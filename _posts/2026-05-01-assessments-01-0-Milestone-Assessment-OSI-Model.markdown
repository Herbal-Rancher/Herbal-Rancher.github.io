## Overview

This milestone assessment explains the structure, function, and importance of the OSI model in modern networking environments.

The assessment examines how each OSI layer contributes to communication, troubleshooting, encapsulation, and interoperability between networking devices and protocols.

---

## Assessment Focus

* OSI model structure and functions
* Encapsulation and de-encapsulation
* Layer-based troubleshooting
* Networking interoperability
* Communication standards and protocols
