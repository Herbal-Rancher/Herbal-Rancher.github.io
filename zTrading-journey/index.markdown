This section documents my process of learning trading and stock trading through the TRADEway Trading Program, personal study, chart practice, paper trading, and trade reviews.

My goal is not to showcase perfect trades, butinstead to document my learning process, mistakes, lessons learned, and progress toward becoming a disciplined tradeor. Also to build understanding, confidence, discipline, and consistent decision-making through a repeatable system.
